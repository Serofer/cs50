sort1 uses: Bubblesort

How do you know?: It is very fast with a sorted list: O(n). But very slow with a reversed list.

sort2 uses: Mergesort

How do you know?: It is by far the fastest and it takes always the same amount of time.

sort3 uses: Selectionsort

How do you know?: It is relatively slow and it takes always similar amount of time: Always O(n^2)
